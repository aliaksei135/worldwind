CSV files containing a representation of terrain obstacles (static & untraversable)

Each line represents a new terrain obstcale obstacle.

To define an obstacle the following must be given:

 - 2 coordinates (with lat and lon) representing the vertexes of an edge of the obstacle box;

 - the left and rightwidth of the obstacle measured prependicullarly from the defined edge;

 - the maximum and minimum heights of the obstacle.

E.g. : lat0, lon0, lat1, lon1, left, right, bottom, top
